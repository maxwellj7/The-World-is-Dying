What Would the Outcome be as a result of Massive Bullying?

* When someone has been constantly bullied for a large amount of time, or at some cases, daily, what could happen to the target in question are the following...

* The victim will feel afraid of the bully. The bully's goal is to make the victim miserable, which in turn would make the bullying worse as a result.

* The victim's physical health is likely to dwindle over time. The victim of the bullying is also vunurable to developing mental health problems such as depression, low self-esteem, anxiety and even develop suicidal thoughts.

* The person inflicted may also be likely of dropping out of school because of bullying.
